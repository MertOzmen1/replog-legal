RepLog: Workout Tracker — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

RepLog: Workout Tracker (“App”, “RepLog”) is a mobile application that helps you track your workouts and fitness data. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App, how it is used, and your rights.

App developer: MNcode (“we”, “us”, “our”) Package name: com.mncode.replog.app

DATA WE COLLECT
Depending on how you use RepLog, the following data may be processed:

1.1. Data stored on your device (all users)

You can use the App in guest mode or without creating an account. In that case, the following data is stored only in the local database (SQLite) on your device:

Workout records (date, exercises, sets, weights, reps, duration, notes)
Cardio sessions
Personal records (PRs)
Body measurements (weight, height, body measurements, gender, notes)
Custom exercise definitions
App preferences (theme, units, community display name, etc.)
1.2. Account and authentication data (optional)

When you create an account or sign in, the following information is processed through Firebase Authentication:

Email address
Password (stored securely by Firebase; we cannot see your password)
If you use Google Sign-In: basic profile information linked to your Google account (name, email, profile photo URL)
Optional display name
Unique user ID (UID)
Supported sign-in methods: email/password and Google Sign-In.

1.3. Community (Q&A) data

An account is required to use the community section. Content you submit is stored on Google Firebase Firestore and can be read by other users:

Question, answer, and comment text
Your community display name
Like and interaction records (linked to your UID)
Creation date
1.4. RepLog Pro cloud backup (optional)

With a RepLog Pro subscription and account sign-in, you can choose to back up your workout data to the cloud. In that case, the fitness data described in section 1.1 is stored in Firebase Firestore, linked only to your account.

1.5. Purchase data

RepLog Pro subscription transactions are processed through Google Play. Payment information (credit card, etc.) is not collected directly by us; it is processed by Google Play. Subscription status may be verified using information from Google Play.

1.6. Advertising data

The free version displays Google AdMob ads. Google may collect device identifiers, IP address, ad interactions, and similar technical data for ad delivery and measurement under its own privacy policy.

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
To provide workout logging and statistics features
To manage account creation, sign-in, and password reset
To operate the community Q&A feature
To provide RepLog Pro cloud backup and synchronization
To verify subscription status
To display ads in the free version
To maintain app security (Firebase App Check)
To fix errors and improve the service
THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
The App uses the following third-party services:

Google Firebase — authentication, database (Firestore), security (App Check)
Google Sign-In — sign in with Google account
Google AdMob — ad display
Google Play — app distribution and in-app purchases
These service providers have their own privacy policies:

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Firebase Privacy and Security: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
DATA SHARING
We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the service providers listed above, to the extent necessary for the App to function. Questions, answers, and comments you share in the community section may be visible to other users.

DATA RETENTION
Device data: Remains on your device until you uninstall the App or use “Delete all data”.
Account data: Stored in Firebase while your account is active.
Cloud backup: Stored in Firestore while RepLog Pro backup is enabled.
Community content: May be retained until you request deletion or when moderation is required.
DELETING YOUR DATA AND YOUR RIGHTS
You can manage your data in the following ways:

Local data: Settings → Delete all data
Account: Settings → Sign out; contact us to fully delete your account
Cloud backup: Cancel your Pro subscription and request account deletion
Community content: Contact us to request deletion
Under applicable data protection laws, including the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK), you have the right to access, correct, request deletion of, and object to the processing of your data. You may send requests to the contact address below.

SECURITY
We use technical measures such as Firebase security rules, encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), and access controls so that only authenticated users can access their own cloud backups. No system is 100% secure; absolute security cannot be guaranteed despite reasonable safeguards.

CHILDREN’S PRIVACY
RepLog is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided us with data, they may contact us and we will delete the relevant information.

INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS
Firebase and Google services may process your data on servers outside Turkey (for example, in the European Union or the United States). Google provides appropriate security and contractual safeguards for data transfers.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will update the “Last updated” date. The current version will always be available at the link you provide.
